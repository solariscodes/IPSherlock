The Case File: About IPSherlock

IPSherlock is your digital detective agency, specialized in uncovering the mysteries behind IP addresses and domain names. Like a skilled investigator, we reveal the hidden clues and connections in the digital world that others might miss.

The Detective's Oath

Our mission is to provide a powerful investigative toolkit that makes the complex world of network forensics accessible to everyone - from cybersecurity professionals to curious digital citizens seeking answers.

Our Investigation Tools

  • IP Address Lookup: Get detailed information about any IP address including geolocation, ISP, organization, and more.
  • Domain Investigation: Uncover registration details, DNS records, and hosting information for any domain name.
  • WHOIS Data: Access comprehensive WHOIS records with registrar information, creation dates, and contact details.
  • DNS Records: View all DNS records including A, AAAA, MX, TXT, NS, and more.
  • Data Export: Download investigation results in CSV format for further analysis or record-keeping.

The Investigation Process

IPSherlock employs a network of informants (reliable data sources and APIs) to gather intelligence about IP addresses and domains. Our forensic system analyzes this evidence and presents a comprehensive case file in an organized, easy-to-understand format with our signature detective approach.

Confidentiality Guarantee

We operate with the utmost discretion. IPSherlock doesn't maintain records of your investigations or personal information. All case work is conducted in real-time and findings are delivered directly to you without being archived in our evidence room.

The Art of Digital Investigation

Cases We Solve

Our digital detective work serves many critical purposes in today's interconnected world:

  • Cybersecurity: Identifying the source of suspicious traffic or potential threats
  • Website Verification: Confirming the legitimacy of websites before sharing sensitive information
  • Email Security: Verifying the source of emails by examining domain records
  • Network Diagnostics: Troubleshooting connectivity issues by examining network paths
  • Digital Forensics: Gathering evidence for investigations into online activities

Our Detective Methods

We employ a sophisticated array of investigative techniques to crack each case:

  • WHOIS Databases: Querying domain registration records from global registrars
  • DNS Resolution: Examining the Domain Name System to understand how domains are configured
  • Geolocation Services: Mapping IP addresses to physical locations around the world
  • ASN Lookups: Identifying the networks and organizations that manage IP address blocks
  • Reverse DNS: Finding hostnames associated with IP addresses
