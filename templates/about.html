About IPSherlock

IPSherlock is a comprehensive tool designed to help users investigate and gather detailed information about IP addresses and domain names. Just like a detective, IPSherlock uncovers the hidden details behind network identifiers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple yet powerful interface for network investigations, making technical information accessible to everyone from IT professionals to curious individuals.

Key Features

  • IP Address Lookup: Get detailed information about any IP address including geolocation, ISP, organization, and more.
  • Domain Investigation: Uncover registration details, DNS records, and hosting information for any domain name.
  • WHOIS Data: Access comprehensive WHOIS records with registrar information, creation dates, and contact details.
  • DNS Records: View all DNS records including A, AAAA, MX, TXT, NS, and more.
  • Data Export: Download investigation results in CSV format for further analysis or record-keeping.

How It Works

IPSherlock uses multiple reliable data sources and APIs to gather and compile information about IP addresses and domains. Our system processes this data and presents it in an organized, easy-to-understand format with a detective-themed interface.

Privacy & Security

We respect your privacy. IPSherlock doesn't store search queries or personal information. All lookups are performed in real-time and results are displayed directly to you without being saved on our servers.
