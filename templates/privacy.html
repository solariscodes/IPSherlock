Confidential Case Files: Privacy Policy

At IPSherlock Detective Agency, we treat your information with the same confidentiality we apply to our most sensitive cases. This dossier explains our handling protocols for your data.

Evidence Gathered

We collect only essential clues to conduct our digital investigations:

  • Investigation Queries: The IP addresses or domain names you submit
  • Session Data: Basic information required for our detective tools to function
  • Device Information: Browser type and version to optimize your experience

Digital Fingerprints (Cookies)

We employ essential digital tracking tools to maintain case continuity:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your investigation settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us improve our detective methods

Our Network of Informants

To deliver accurate investigation results, we consult with trusted sources:

  • WHOIS Databases: Uncover domain ownership records and registration details
  • IP Geolocation Services: Track digital footprints to physical locations
  • DNS Services: Decode the aliases and technical configurations of digital entities
  • Threat Intelligence: Identify potentially malicious IP addresses or domains

Case File Security

Your privacy is our top priority in all investigations:

  • No Archives Policy: We don't store your investigation history
  • Secure Processing: All temporary case materials are protected with advanced security protocols
  • Data Minimization: We only collect what's necessary to solve your case
  • No Data Sharing: Your information is never sold to third parties

IPSherlock Detective Agency is committed to maintaining the confidentiality of all your digital investigations. If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact our Chief Privacy Detective.
